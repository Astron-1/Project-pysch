ER-STEPS, short for Emotion Regulation Skills for Teens, Parents, Schools, is a web-based mental health promotion and prevention program. It contains educational videos for parents, teens, and teachers to learn ways to enhance their mental health and wellbeing. The program is skill-focused and teaches you how to handle emotions in a variety of different situations.

In this program, you will be required to watch a 20-40 min video once a week for four weeks, as well as fill out survey forms and worksheets. The exact dates, as well as additional details, will be informed to you once you are selected for the study.

Irrespective of the group, all participants will receive gift vouchers up to Rs. 1800 to compensate for the time they invest in the study.
